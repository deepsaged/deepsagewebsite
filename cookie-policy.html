1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device (computer, tablet, phone) by your web browser. They help websites remember your preferences and improve your browsing experience. Cookies do not contain viruses or malware.

2. How We Use Cookies

  • Improve site performance and user experience
  • Measure the effectiveness of marketing campaigns

4. Managing Your Cookies

Via Our Cookie Banner

You can manage your cookie preferences anytime by clicking the cookie preferences link in the banner at the bottom of our website.

Via Your Browser

You can also control cookies through your browser settings:

  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy and Security → Cookies and other site data
  • Firefox: Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Cookies and website data
  • Edge: Settings → Privacy → Clear browsing data → Cookies and other site data

Disable All Cookies

You can disable all cookies in your browser, but this may affect website functionality and user experience.

5. Do Not Track

Some browsers include a "Do Not Track" feature. Our website currently does not respond to Do Not Track signals, but you can always use browser settings to control cookie usage.

6. Third-Party Cookies

Our website uses Google Analytics, which may set cookies from Google. You can opt-out of Google Analytics tracking by:

  • Using our cookie consent banner to disable Analytics cookies
  • Installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on
  • Adjusting your browser settings
