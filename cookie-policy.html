Cookie Policy

Last updated: July 12, 2026

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device by your web browser. They help websites remember preferences and improve browsing experience. Cookies do not contain viruses or malware.

2. How We Use Cookies

  • Remember your cookie preferences
  • Understand how you use our website
  • Improve site performance and user experience
  • Measure marketing campaign effectiveness

3. Cookie Categories

Help us understand how you use the website.

Track interactions to show targeted ads.

Purpose Retention

4. Managing Your Cookies

Via Our Banner

Manage preferences anytime using our cookie banner.

Via Your Browser

  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy → Cookies and site data
  • Firefox: Settings → Privacy → Cookies and Site Data
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Cookies and data
  • Edge: Settings → Privacy → Browsing data → Cookies

5. Third-Party Cookies

We use Google Analytics which may set cookies from Google. Opt-out options:

  • Use our cookie banner to disable Analytics
  • Install Google Analytics Opt-out Add-on
  • Adjust your browser settings
